# EGGSISTENTIAL

**Eggsistential** is a high-stakes, skill-based arcade game built on the Solana blockchain. It combines the addictive "just one more try" gameplay of classics like Frogger with the transparency and thrill of on-chain competition.

## The Vision

In Eggsistential, your skill directly impacts your rewards. No black boxes, no hidden odds—just you, a chicken, and a dangerous road. Cross lanes, hit checkpoints, and build your reputation as an elite runner.

## Key Features

- **Skill-First Gameplay**: Your success depends on your timing and reflexes, not just luck.
- **Social Login**: Jump into the game instantly using your Google account or existing Solana wallet via Reown AppKit.
- **On-Chain Reputation (EggPass)**: Earn your way through tiers by reaching difficult checkpoints. Your achievements are stored on-chain, proving your skills to the world.
- **Real-Time Competition**: Compete for the top spot on the global leaderboard.
- **Secure Settlements**: Every win and loss is settled securely on the Solana blockchain.

## How to Play

1. **Connect**: Sign in with your favorite social account or Solana wallet.
2. **Fund**: Use the faucet to get test tokens or deposit your own to start a run.
3. **Run**: Navigate your chicken across the lanes. Each lane increases your multiplier.
4. **Cash Out**: Reach a checkpoint and decide: do you take your winnings now, or push for the next tier?
5. **Level Up**: Earn your **EggPass** by consistently performing at high levels.

## Project Structure

For developers and contributors, the project is divided into three main parts:

- **/frontend**: The web interface (Next.js), where the action happens.
- **/backend**: The game engine and authentication service (Node.js).
- **/sc**: The heart of the game—the Solana smart contracts (Anchor/Rust).
