# Passport

## Overview

Passport is the reputation layer of EGGSISTENTIAL.

It turns gameplay progression into a wallet-linked trust signal that can be useful inside the product and across other applications.

## What Passport Represents

Passport is meant to reflect:

- consistency
- progression
- repeated engagement
- believable player history

## Why Passport Exists

Many wallet reputation systems focus on ownership, volume, or visibility.

Passport takes a different approach by focusing on behavior.

The core idea is simple:

- the game is the proving ground
- the Passport is the reusable result

## Where Passport Is Useful

### Inside EGGSISTENTIAL

- progression identity
- player status
- future unlocks or access layers

### Outside EGGSISTENTIAL

- allowlist qualification
- partner rewards
- gated experiences
- user quality filtering

## Long-Term Role

The long-term goal of Passport is to become a lightweight trust primitive for Solana consumer products that care about stronger user quality and more meaningful participation.
